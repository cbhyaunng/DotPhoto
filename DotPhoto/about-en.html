About

Dot Photo Studio is a web app that converts images into dot-style art directly in your browser.

Purpose

The service helps users quickly create dot-art results by processing uploaded images locally in the browser, without mandatory server-side upload.

Core Features

  • Image upload and drag-and-drop
  • Dot size, color levels, and dithering controls
  • Before/After comparison slider
  • Palette extraction with color code preview
  • PNG export for converted output

Operating Principles

  • Uploaded images are processed locally in the browser by default.
  • Service policies are publicly available on the site.
  • Bug reports and improvement requests are accepted through the contact channel.
Service information and policy documents are available publicly on this website.
